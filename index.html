Who We Are

About Nilolabs

A Nairobi-based technology company building the communication infrastructure for Africa's digital economy.

Nilolabs Technologies specializes in messaging infrastructure, conversational AI, and embedded systems. We help businesses — from startups to enterprises — deploy intelligent communication channels that drive engagement and revenue.

Our flagship product, Msaidizi, is a WhatsApp-based assistant platform designed for African businesses. It handles customer support, order tracking, appointment scheduling, and more — all through the messaging app your customers already use.

We believe technology should remove friction, not add it. Every solution we build is designed to be simple, reliable, and genuinely useful.
